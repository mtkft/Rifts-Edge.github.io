# About
From Mochi:
"Worldbuilding ideas: Craesia

History:
The land to the West of the Carin estate was basically a no man’s land due to the harsh weather conditions and its terrain, only rarely used as a hideout for criminals who were desperate enough to escape the estates. 

It was until one giant snake god bish got clocked and died on a mountain or smth. After some time, say a few years depending on the timeline had caught the attention of some alchemists, or whatever the equivalent is who just looked at the body and were like, it’s free real estate ( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°) And later named the mountain Calvaria Dei or God’s Skull after the decaying snake corpse.

The alchemists quickly arranged to make small outposts near the foot of the mountain as a temporary base to study the fallen God’s anatomy. However, what was supposed to be a short expedition with only a handful of some elite peeps, turned into a bigass settlement full of other alchemists who came either for the remains or were family members, partners etc. who came along with them. 

Once the settlements turned into an actual country after rapid development, the lead alchemists decided on the name Craesia after the snake skull as they first seeked refuge in said skull when they first arrived in the area.

Over time, the people who lived in the settlements slowly acquired magic due to how close they lived next to the corpse, and were surviving off of any creatures that still lived on the mountain. Essentially just the magic version of bioaccumulation. 

In terms of technology, they rely more on magic as they didn’t really see a need to use machinery when they have a powerful source of magic to draw from. Essentially a country where magic is basically every part of their life, and looks down especially on the Pavek estate over their proficiency in tech."
